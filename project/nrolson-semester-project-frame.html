The Definitive Jaguar X308 Guide

The legendary Jaguar XJ series is the manufacturer's finest product, enduring a life span of over 50 years. The XJ peaked with the magnificent X308 generation, a vehicle that blends a timeless design with Jaguar's most revolutionary powertrain. If you're in love with the finest XJ of the breed, this website was built for you.


What makes the X308?

Style

The X308 is timeless. Penned by Geoff Lawson, the design has tryly withstood the test of time. People who see them on the street have a difficult time guessing the car's age.

Luxury

The X308 features a sumptuous interior that's swathed in wood and leather. Although the newest examples are over 15 years old, they are still well appointed with features such as parking sensors, electricaly ajustable steering column, automaic wipers, and automatic headlights

Performance

The X308 is quick in both nautrally aspirated and supercharged forms. Although the suspension tuning is different between standard and R models, they all strike a balance between sport and comfort

Value

Prices for a mint XJR top out at $10,000, with most X308s sitting around the $4,000 mark for a decent example. The X308 is one of the most reliable XJ models which, combined with all of the attributes above, makes it an affordable luxury bargain.
